Course Planner for SPH | Mar 9, 2021

Help biostatistics students to create a sample of courses schedule

This project aims to help our client: the University of Michigan School of Public Health to build a course planner application to help students to schedule their classes. And even attract more prospective students with this interactive tool which helps them to see what a semester or year might look like at the School of Public Health.

Our goal is to help our client to define the problem, decide related features, wireframe the UX layout on the various portal of their website, prototype hi-fi UI design, and finally build it with engineers and data scientists.
